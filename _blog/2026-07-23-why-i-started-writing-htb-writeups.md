Preivously I used to have an obsidian vault filled with various writeups, notes, cheat sheets but realized It became overwhelming and tied down to a single application. I did see alot of benefits and it was better than just having a word doc or a bunch of txt files but most of the time I just needed a quick recap. I decided to start this site as a way not only to share some of my notes and progress as a portfolio but for writeups a dedicate site like this that I can customize feels better.
